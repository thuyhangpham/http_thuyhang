## Interactive Dashboard Built in Power BI

As part of this project, I designed and built multiple **interactive dashboards** using **Power BI** to support data-driven decisions in the **Purchasing Department** of ***Wide World Importers (WWI)***. Below are the main dashboards included in the solution:
### 1.  Purchase Invoices Dashboard

- **Purpose:** Provide a high-level overview of procurement performance and supplier spending.

- **Key Insight:**

  Total number of purchase documents and items ordered.

  Total purchase amount and cost.

  Spending distribution by product group and supplier.

  Top vendors by total spending.
  
![Dashboard for Purchase Invoices](https://github.com/thuyhangpham/http_thuyhang/blob/20d1c7e409871419533f6f42f714742fb3d96019/Cloud-based%20BI%20Solution%20for%20Purchasing%20Department/Visualization/Dashboard_Purchase%20Invoices.png)

### 2.  Purchase Invoices Details Dashboard

- **Purpose:** Analyze detailed purchasing behavior over time and across different dimensions.

- **Key Insight:**

  Monthly purchase trends (comparison between current and previous year).

  Purchase breakdown by product group and city.

  Average spending per supplier.

![Dashboard for Purchase Invoices Details](https://github.com/thuyhangpham/http_thuyhang/blob/cdbb44e4861d92e398822b6a54c19331b99fc065/Cloud-based%20BI%20Solution%20for%20Purchasing%20Department/Visualization/Dashboard_Purchase%20Invoices%20Details.png)

### 3.  Purchase Orders Dashboard

- **Purpose:** Track purchase order execution and supplier delivery performance.

- **Key Insight:**

  Fulfillment and cancellation rates.

  Average processing and delivery time.
  
  On-time delivery rate by supplier.

![Dashboard for Purchase Invoices Details](https://github.com/thuyhangpham/http_thuyhang/blob/20d1c7e409871419533f6f42f714742fb3d96019/Cloud-based%20BI%20Solution%20for%20Purchasing%20Department/Visualization/Dashboard_Purchase%20Orders.png)

### 4.   Stock Management Dashboard

- **Purpose:** Evaluate inventory quality and monitor defective stock rates.

- **Key Insight:**

  Defect rate by supplier.
  
  Inventory levels by product.

  Historical receipt of goods over time.

![Dashboard for Stock](https://github.com/thuyhangpham/http_thuyhang/blob/20d1c7e409871419533f6f42f714742fb3d96019/Cloud-based%20BI%20Solution%20for%20Purchasing%20Department/Visualization/Dashboard_Stock%20Management.png)

### 5.   Vendor Analysis Dashboard

- **Purpose:** Assess supplier performance and identify opportunities for cost optimization.

- **Key Insight:**

  Top 5 suppliers by total spending.

  Average procurement cost per supplier.

  Defective rates and delivery reliability.
